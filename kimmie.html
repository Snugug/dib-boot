Kimmie's Fantabulous Page

In 1970-something, my father— fighter pilot— escaped Vietnam. He ditched his uniform and snuck onto a civilian air craft, which took him to Guam and then America. He accidentally reunited with his wife and my mother in California.

He says I owe my existence to luck and his ingenuity.

I celebrate that by going to art school in America, drawing aquatic life and taking photos of cityscapes. I think he would say "Life is beautiful, you gotta look around."

He would also tell me I could be working harder.
