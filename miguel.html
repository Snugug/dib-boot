It's all about ME!

My full name is Captain Awesomeness Migs Shufflebottom, I'm named after my uncle the famous bounty hunter., you won't have heard of him since all evidence of his work was covered up during the cold war. In my spare time I enjoy the touch of the wind on my face, walks on the beach and extensive conversations. I would live to share my favorite quote with you: "Friendship is like peeing on yourself: everyone can see it, but only you get the warm feeling that it brings." ~Unknown

Some more stuff about ME

I always try to cheer myself up by singing when I get down. But most of the time, it turns out that my voice is worse than my problems. I consider myself a crayon, I might not be your favorite color but one day you'll need me to complete your picture. When I die, I want to go peacefully like my Grandfather did, in his sleep - not screaming, like the passengers in his car.
