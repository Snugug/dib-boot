Boring Version

Pinguino is a mulidisciplined artist and event promoter who has worked in web since it started. At 10 yrs old she was running a comic publishing company called Penguin Palace. Her first "real job" was at the first Internet bank that worked with an early form of digital currency in 1995. Her work in photography has been published by Comic Book Resources, Anime News Network, Marvel, MTV, and Playboy. Her most recent art show piece was part of the Doctor Who 50th Anniversary show in Hollywood. Events she's hosted include the We Heart Japan fundraising series, Super Happy Tokyo Love, NinjaPenguin, Penguin Project, and Junglist Movement. She's a prolific artist and travels around the world on caffeine-fueled adventures.
