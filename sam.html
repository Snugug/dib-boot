Sam Richard

About Me

Sam, better known as Snugug throughout the Internet, is a Front End Developer who waxes philosophical about Sass, Compass, Responsive Web Design, Design in Browser, Content Strategy, and User Experience. He is the author of North, a set of modern web development best practices, as well as a number of tools to make authoring CSS, designing in browser, rapid prototyping, and building high performance, responsive sites easier.

Speaking

Sam has spoken at a number of local, national, and international camps and conferences, including CSSDevConf 2013, Future of Web Design NY 2013, DrupalCon Munich, DrupalCon Portland, and DrupalCon Prague, and BADCamp 2012 and 2013.
